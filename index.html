Features

Discover what make us special

Community

A strong community made up of people from every horizon.

Multiple text and voice channels to banter about everything or just play with your friends.

An open-minded staff who listen and care about your requests and suggestions.

No fees, no cost, all you need is a Discord account to join us.

Games

We're currently hosting  CS:GO and  GMod servers with multiple game modes such as Deathmatch, Hide and Seek, Death Run...

We're also hosting sessions of DrawSomething, Cards against Humanity and TV Show / Anime with Rabb.it.

More games are planned and suggestions are welcome.

Music & Radio

We created custom bots for Discord, allowing you to play songs of your choice from  YouTube and  SoundCloud.

Along with that, we have multiple voice channels where you can play with your friends and listen to constant radio broadcast in the background.

All the music bots are hosted in UK with a 5ms delay to Discord voice servers, allowing for a crystal clear and lag-free audio experience.

Stability

Our global services are hosted by Discord and  GitHub and are therefore guaranted to stay online, no matter what happens, as long as theses services are running.

Our staff is exclusively composed of experienced administrators and moderators.

All our game servers are hosted in Western Europe and covered with uptime guarantee, along with DDOS protection.

About us

Learn more about Midgård

Midgård started as a small Discord community formed of friends with a common passion for memes and games.

As time passed, we started growing, inviting friends we play with, gathering together to share our passion.

After gaining some maturity, we decided to open the doors of our safe place to the public. Come and get a taste of it now!
