Features

Discover what make us special

Community

A strong community made of people from every horizons.

Multiple text and voice channels to banter about everything or just play with your friends.

An open-minded staff who listen and cares about your requests and suggestions.

No fees, no cost, all you need is a Discord account to join us.

Games

We're currently hosting CS:GO and GMod servers with multiple game modes such as Deathmatch, Hide and Seek, Death run...

We're also hosting sessions of DrawSomething, Cards against Humanity and TV Show / Anime with Rabb.it.

More games are planned and suggestions are welcome.

Music & Radio

We created custom bots for Discord allowing you to play songs of your choice from youtube or soundcloud.

Along with that we have multiple voice channels where you can play with your friends and listen to constant radio broadcast in background.

All the music bots are hosted in UK with a 5ms delay to Discord servers, allowing a crystal clear and lag free audio experience.

Stability

Global services are hosted by respectively Discord and Github, and therefore guaranted to stay online no matter what happens as long as theses services are running.

Our staff is exclusively composed of experienced administrators and moderators.

All our game servers are hosting in western europe and covered with uptime guarantees along with DDOS protections.

About us

Learn more about Midgård

Midgård started as a small Discord community formed of friends with a common passion for memes and games.

As time passed we started growing, inviting friends we play with and gather all together to share our passion.

After gaining some maturity, we decided to open the doors of our safe place to the public. Come and get a taste of it now !
